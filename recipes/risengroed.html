Risengroed

How to make risengroed

Here you will learn all the steps to make risengroed!

Risengroed is a traditional Scandinavian Yuletide dish enjoyed by humans and tomter alike

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Rice
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook water and rice in a large cauldron
  2. Add milk and boil
  3. Add salt
  4. The risengroed is finished!
  5. Serve with cinnamon, sugar, and a big chunk of butter
